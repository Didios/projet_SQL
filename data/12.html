12.Quelle sont les 5 comedies romantiques les mieux notees ? 

SELECT originalTitle
FROM title_basics
JOIN title_ratings 
ON title_ratings.tconst = title_basics.tconst 
WHERE genres 
LIKE "%Romance%"
AND genres 
LIKE "%Comedy%"
ORDER BY averageRating 
DESC
LIMIT 5
-------------------------------------------------------------

Super Bowl Sunday
The Last One
The One Where Everybody Finds Out
The Cabin
The Stockholm Syndrome
