18.Quels sont les titres des films les plus connus de Sean Connery ? 

SELECT originalTitle
FROM title_basics
JOIN name_titles 
ON knownForTitles = tconst 
JOIN name_basics 
ON name_basics.nconst = name_titles.nconst 
WHERE primaryName = "Sean Connery"
AND titleType = "movie"
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Goldfinger
The Rock
The Untouchables
Thunderball
